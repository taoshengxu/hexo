表达标签序列（Express sequence tag, ESTs）

来自随机选取的cDNA克隆末端序列。一个EST就是对应于某一种mRNA的一个cDna克隆的一端序列。

NCBI EST数据库（dbEST）

What is dbEST?

EST sequences are included in the EST division of GenBank, available from NCBI through E-Utilities. EST sequences are also available by anonymous ftp in the /repository/dbEST directory at ftp.ncbi.nih.gov

Please note that ESTs received through the end of 2018 are available by FTP at ftp.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/repository/dbEST
ESTs received after 2018 will not be included in this format.
